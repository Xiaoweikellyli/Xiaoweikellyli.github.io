TAKE ACTION

    REUSE REDUCE RECYCLE

  • Use reusable things such as a reusable water bottle.

  • Stop using plastic straws, plastic bags, etc.

  • Recycle plastics, and other materials.

  • Go to this website to learn more about the 3 Rs.

    • SAVE ENERGY

  • Close the lights when you don’t need it to conserve energy.

  • Unplug things you are not using.

  • Go to this website for more examples of things you can do to help save energy!

    • VOLUNTEER

  • Do you want to be even more involved by volunteering, and you live in NYC? Then, this website is the perfect fit for you! Look for something interesting you might want to do and something you have time for, then GO FOR IT! Link to Volunteer

  • If you don’t live in NYC and also want to volunteer, try searching up parks near you to see if they offer volunteer opportunities!

  • You can also join a school club that is based on helping the environment, or if your school doesn’t already have a environmental club, you can always create one! Tell all your friends, and classmates to join to spread awareness! Through this club, you will also be able to volunteer, and with friends too!
