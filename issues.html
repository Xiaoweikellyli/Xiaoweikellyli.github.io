SO, WHAT ARE THESE ISSUES?

Air Pollution

  • Causes: The use of fossil fuels, emissions from the industries, automobiles, and more.

  • Effects: Increased risk of diseases, and also adds to global warming.

    • Deforestation

  • Causes: The cutting down of trees to have more farmland, and land to build on.

  • Problem: Adds to the Environmental issues, because trees, that reduces carbon emissions, are being cut down.

  • Effects: Increases carbon emissions, meaning more air pollution.

    • Global warming

  • Causes: Air pollution, greenhouse gases, deforestation, farming livestock, and more.

  • Effects: Earthâ€™s temperature increases, causing sea ice to melt, which affects animals that depend on it, such as polar bears, walruses, arctic foxes, and snowy owls.

    • Climate Change

  • Causes: Greenhouse gases

  • Effects: Environmental changes such as global warming, flooding, forest fires, change in seasons, and epidemics occurring.

    • Water Pollution

  • Causes: Harmful substances from companies are being dumped into rivers, oceans, and lakes and ponds.

  • Effects: Organisms in the contaminated body of water dies.

    • Ocean Acidification

  • Causes: The increase of carbon dioxide in the air from air pollution.

  • Effects: The amount of oxygen in the water to decrease. Therefore, the organisms in the bodies of water die.
